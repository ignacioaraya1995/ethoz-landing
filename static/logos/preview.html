Concept 1: The Guardian

Enterprise / Trust

A solid shield with a compliance checkmark. Directly communicates Ethoz's core promise: "El escudo digital de tu comunidad escolar." The blue gradient conveys authority and trust. Best for an audience of school administrators and decision-makers.

Concept 2: The Network

Tech / Community

A hexagonal shape (stability, structure) with three connected nodes representing the school community: students, staff, and parents. The blue-to-emerald gradient transitions from trust to growth. Feels like a modern SaaS product.

Concept 3: The Keystone

Minimal / Modern

A bold geometric E in a rounded square. Instantly app-icon ready. Matches your current favicon approach but elevated with precise geometric proportions. Think Notion, Linear, or Vercel. The most versatile option across all sizes and contexts.

Combination Mark

Concept 4: The Fusion

Creative / Distinctive

A shield outline (blue = trust) houses a solid E letterform (emerald = growth). The two-tone approach tells the brand story: Ethoz is the protective structure that lets schools thrive. The most unique and recognizable option of the four.
